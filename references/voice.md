# Voice Reference

Match this register when drafting. Casual but direct. Short sentences. Punchy. Builds an argument by stacking observations. No fluff, no corporate speak. Uses contrast to land a point ("You stop buying a filing cabinet. You start buying oversight."). Comfortable mixing English and Afrikaans depending on audience.

Don't fake my voice on external content (LinkedIn, email to clients) without showing me a draft first.
