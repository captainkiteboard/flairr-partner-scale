## The Product: Board Pack Intelligence System

### What It Is (One Line)
An AI system that turns disconnected board reporting into connected governance intelligence — decisions linked to risks, actions linked to owners, accountability visible in real time.

### What It's NOT
- Not another board portal (Diligent, OnBoard, BoardEffect own that)
- Not a document storage system
- Not a meeting scheduler
- Not a filing cabinet with better UX

### What It IS
**An intelligence layer that sits on top of existing tools** and connects what they can't:

### The Core Capabilities

**1. Decision Intelligence Graph**
Every board decision gets tracked with:
- What was decided
- Why it was decided (evidence, context, risks considered)
- Who owns the outcome
- What actions it triggered
- What risks it relates to
- What the status is now
- What changed since the last review

Not a list. A **connected graph.** Pull on any decision and see everything it touches.

**2. Accountability Tracker**
- Every action item assigned to an owner with a deadline
- Automated follow-up when deadlines approach or pass
- Escalation when items go unresolved across multiple board cycles
- Visual dashboard: who's accountable for what, what's overdue, what's drifting

**3. Governance Drift Detection**
AI monitors patterns across board cycles:
- "This risk was flagged 3 quarters ago. No action has been taken."
- "This decision's owner changed twice. No handoff notes exist."
- "This KPI has been declining for 4 months. It wasn't mentioned in the last 2 board packs."
- "This action item has been 'in progress' for 6 months."

**Surfaces what nobody is watching.** That's the intelligence layer that doesn't exist today.

**4. Board Pack Assembly Engine**
- Pulls data from existing sources (financials, project tools, CRM, previous minutes)
- Auto-generates structured board pack sections
- Highlights what changed since last pack
- Flags items that need board attention vs. items that are FYI
- **Cuts preparation from 4-6 weeks to 1-2 days**

**5. Audit Trail & Evidence Chain**
- Every decision has a full evidence chain
- Compliance-ready: who decided, when, based on what
- Searchable: "show me every decision related to [topic] in the last 2 years"
- Exportable: ready for external audit, regulatory review, or due diligence

**6. Board Intelligence Dashboard**
- Real-time view: open actions, overdue items, upcoming decisions, risk status
- Trend analysis: governance health over time
- Comparison: this quarter vs. last quarter
- Board member prep: "here's what you need to know before Thursday's meeting" in 5 minutes, not 4 hours
