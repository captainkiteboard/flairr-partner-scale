# About the Business

Flairr is an AI automation agency that builds practical, high-ROI systems for SMEs. Not generic AI tools — custom implementations that automate real business processes: document workflows, customer onboarding, support systems, data pipelines, credit interpretation, and more.

## Core Offerings

- **AI Agents & Knowledge Systems** — RAG-based knowledge hubs, support assistants (email, WhatsApp, SMS, voice), AI audits
- **Workflow & Document Automation** — AIOS builds, onboarding automation, document collection/validation, PDF extraction, credit interpretation
- **Data & Custom Builds** — data consolidation + NL query, database reactivation campaigns, custom dashboards

## Revenue Model

Mix of project-based builds (fixed fee or hourly), per-unit usage fees (per document, per interpretation), monthly retainers, and revenue-share partnerships. Moving toward more MRR. Revenue lands in Revolut Business; moving to Zoho Books.

## ICP

Small-to-medium businesses that need custom AI implementations — not off-the-shelf tools. Active clients: Adlib, Maxlaw, Optron, OTB Tax, Traffico, TravelKinship. Some sourced through agency partnerships (ClarityFirst, Voyage IQ).

## This AIOS Focus

Research and scale. Finding new opportunities, refining service delivery, growing Flairr's reach, and turning custom builds into scalable offerings.
