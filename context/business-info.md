Business Info
Organization Overview
Flairr is an AI automation agency that builds practical, high-ROI systems for SMEs. The focus is on custom implementations — not generic AI tools — that automate real business processes: document workflows, customer onboarding, support systems, data pipelines, credit interpretation, and more.

The business operates out of Netherlands with some international clients. Revenue is primarily ZAR-denominated for SA clients and USD for international partnerships.
